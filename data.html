Data, Information, Content

The core data of the site are the oral histories and data included on the interactive timeline (dates/events and photos). The oral histories will come from our own interviews with women from Reykjavik and from the interviews conducted by Margaret Willson. Dates/events relevant to the history of women working at sea will be gathered from a historian associated with the Museum. The photos and/or maps will be gathered from the Museum and from the interviewees.
